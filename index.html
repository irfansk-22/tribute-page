A Tribute to Legendary Poet Rahat Indori

Brief Bio About Rahat Indori Sir :

  • Orignal name of Rahat Indori was "Rahat Querushi".
  • He was born in 1950 in Madhya pradesh.
  • His father was milk cloth maker and rahat indori was his forth child.
  • Rahat Inodori got his MA in Urdu literature from Barkatullah University Bhopal in 1975.
  • Rahat Indori was also awarded a PhD in Urdu literature from the Bhoj University of Madhya Pradesh in 1985 for his thesis titled Urdu Main Mushaira.
  • Indori has performed in Mushaira and Kavi Sammelan from last 40 - 45 Years. He has traveled widely internationally to recite poetry.
  • One of his poem "Bulati Hai Magar Jaane Ka Nahi" got viral in TikTok and started trending in Facebook, Twitter and Instagram during 2020 Valentines week.
  • He died on 11 August 2020 as he suffered through cardiac arrest prior to his passing.
